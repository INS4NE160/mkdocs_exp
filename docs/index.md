## About

Rooted is my attempt to learn cybersecurity deliberately.

Instead of scattered notes and forgotten writeups, this site acts as a long-term record of what I study, what I struggle with, and what eventually clicks.

The goal isn't just to "get root" — but to build intuition around why systems fail and how they can be defended.

!!! info "Heads up"
    This is a living document. Notes are rough, things change as my understanding improves, and some pages are still being built out.
